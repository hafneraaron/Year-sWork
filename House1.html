Patio

The patio is made of locally-sourced stone and shaded by grape vines growing across the veranda's roof.

Living Room
Fireplace.

In the center of the back wall is a large fireplace. In the center of the room is an ornate rug, with a chair and sofa on either side. A crystal chandelier hangs down from the ceiling.

Kitchen

Small and simple. This room contains all the necesities of a kitchen in a small but comfortable space, to save room for the important things.

Dining Room

This room is centered around a table that seats eight people. It's windows look out to an oldgrowth tree, and it is tastefully decorated to be warm and inviting.

Restroom

A simple restroom mainly for use by guests, who will most often be on the first floor. Contains only a toilet, no shower or bath.

Indoor Pool Room

This is the indoor pool room. The floor is made of white tile, and the walls are drywall coated in water-resistant paint. It contains a variable-depth pool, a large hot-tub, and two white leather couches.

Pool - Deep End

The pool holds aproximately 90'000 gallons, and can be heated up to 76 degrees Farenheight. There is a diving board on the Northern side of the deep end.

Pool - Shallow End

The pool holds aproximately 90'000 gallons, and can be heated up to 76 degrees Farenheight. The shallow end ramps down from ground level to 6 feet.

Hot Tub

The hot tub is built with underwater benches and Jacuzzi jets. A heated towelrack is built into a nearby wall.
